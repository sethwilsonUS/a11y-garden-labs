Infinite Poetry uses AI to generate original poems across dozens of poetic forms — from sonnets and villanelles to haiku and terza rima. Each poem can be paired with an AI-generated illustration. Browse the archive, discover new styles, and explore the intersection of language models and literary tradition.

## Features

- **A new poem every day** — Fresh AI-generated poems are published daily
- **Diverse topics** — Poems span a wide variety of subjects, with new topics contributed by the community
- **Poetic forms** — An array of poetic styles, from classical forms to modern structures
- **Open source** — Community contributions welcome — add new topics, suggest styles, or contribute features
